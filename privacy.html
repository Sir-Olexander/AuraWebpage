Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aura ("we," "our," or "us"). We provide an AI-powered dating assistant application that helps users navigate conversations and manage their dating interactions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Name and age
  • Gender
  • Personality traits and preferences you select

Target Profiles

You may create profiles for people you are conversing with. This information is stored locally on your device and includes:

  • Name, age, and platform (e.g., Instagram, Tinder, WhatsApp)
  • Relationship status and intent
  • Notes, interests, and important dates
  • Conversation context for AI suggestions

Chat Context

To provide AI-powered suggestions, we process:

  • Messages you paste or upload as screenshots
  • Photos you upload for analysis (e.g., profile pictures, stories)
  • Generated AI suggestions based on context

Usage Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our service:

  • Feature usage patterns
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Outcome tracking (e.g., reply rates, conversation outcomes)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI-powered message suggestions and conversation coaching
  • Track conversation analytics and interest levels
  • Send reminders about important dates (Pro feature)
  • Improve our AI models and user experience
  • Process payments for Pro subscriptions
  • Communicate with you about your account and support requests

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Most of your data is stored locally on your device. Target profiles, conversation context, and personal settings remain on your device.

AI Processing: When you request AI suggestions, your chat context is sent to our AI provider for processing. We do not permanently store this data on our servers. Your messages are processed in real-time and not retained after generating suggestions.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase: Backend infrastructure, authentication, and database services for cloud sync functionality.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing for Pro subscriptions.
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing for subscriptions.
  • AI Provider: Generating message suggestions and analyzing screenshots. Your chat context is sent to these services temporarily for processing.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We do not sell your data to third parties.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export: Export your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of analytics collection.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at sir.olexander@gmail.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account:

  • Local data is immediately removed from your device.
  • Cloud data is scheduled for deletion within 30 days.
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

8. Children's Privacy

Aura is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers are located. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we provide GDPR-compliant data processing. Please contact us for more information about your rights under GDPR.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
